About Ajay Arjun Productions

Ajay Arjun Productions is an independent Tamil film production house dedicated to producing quality cinema rooted in Tamil culture and modern storytelling. Our focus is on:

  • Producing feature films with strong scripts and artistic visions.
  • Discovering and introducing new acting, music and technical talent to Tamil cinema.
  • Collaborating with established directors & composers to deliver memorable cinematic experiences.
  • Organising audio launches, publicity events, and music promotions on digital platforms.

Located in Rajapalayam, Jeyaraj Arjun productions works across Chennai & Tamil Nadu for shoots and post-production.

Producer — Dr. Arjunan (Rajapalayam)

Dr. Arjunan is the driving force behind Ajay Arjun Productions. A producer with deep ties to Rajapalayam district, he is committed to producing films that balance artistic merit with audience accessibility.

Dr. Arjunan's mission:

  1. Introduce and mentor new actors, musicians and technicians from Tamil Nadu.
  2. Back films with strong musical identity — believing music elevates storytelling.
  3. Bring together veteran artists and upcoming talent to create films with heart.

Production Philosophy (detailed)

Ajay Arjun Productions believes in the craft of filmmaking across these pillars:

  • Script-first approach: Financing creative stories, not just stars.
  • Music as narrative: Collaborating with composers to make songs that drive emotion and story.
  • Talent incubation: Casting talented newcomers, offering workshops, and pairing them with experienced crew.
  • Regional authenticity: Making films that honour Tamil cultural landscapes while appealing to wider audiences.

If you are a director, musician, cinematographer, or actor who wants mentorship and production support, please reach out through WhatsApp or the contact form below. Shortlisted talents will be invited for auditions and portfolio reviews.

Music Director — Isaignani Ilaiyaraaja (இசைஞானி இளையராஜா)

Ilaiyaraaja (widely known as "Isaignani") is one of India’s most celebrated film composers and orchestral arrangers. For decades he brought a unique blend of Carnatic, folk and western classical elements to Tamil cinema — creating signature songs and background scores that transform scenes into memory.

Cinematographer — Chezhiyan

Chezhiyan is an award-winning director of photography known for his sensitive, naturalistic visuals in films such as Paradesi and other acclaimed Tamil films. His work emphasizes texture, rural landscapes, natural light and human faces — making him a strong visual collaborator for films rooted in place and people.
