About Us

Best AC Lodge is located at 289, Old Bus Stand Road, Bhavani, Erode District – 638 301. Just a few minutes away from the historic Sangameshwarar Temple, our lodge offers the perfect blend of tradition and modern comfort.

We offer well-furnished rooms with attached bathrooms, hot and cold water, television, ample parking facilities, and complimentary Wi-Fi. For special occasions, we provide catering facilities and a spacious Thirumana Hall for weddings and family gatherings.
